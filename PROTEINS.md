# Human Protein Structures & Resources

This document provides a directory of structural and functional resources for the proteins involved in human translation and amino acid metabolism, focusing on high-quality structures from the Protein Data Bank (PDB) and comprehensive data from UniProt and Proteopedia.

## Essential Sources

- **[RCSB Protein Data Bank (PDB)](https://www.rcsb.org/):** The primary global repository for 3D biological macromolecular structures.
- **[UniProt](https://www.uniprot.org/):** The comprehensive resource for protein sequence and functional information.
- **[Proteopedia](https://proteopedia.org/):** An interactive, collaborative 3D-encyclopedia of proteins and other molecules.
- **[AlphaFold Protein Structure Database](https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk/):** Provides high-accuracy predicted structures for nearly all known proteins.
